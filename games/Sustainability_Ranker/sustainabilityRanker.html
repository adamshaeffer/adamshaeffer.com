Sustainability Ranker 5000

Controls:

Z - Start exploring
Arrow Keys - Move the cursor
Hold Z - Make small movements

About the game:

Enjoy this little globe simulation displaying where the 50 cities are placed in the 'Sustainable City Index' (2015). You will also see each city's rankings in the sub-categories: People, Planet, and Profit.
I made this for my creative presentation assignment in my Sustainable Cities class.
