Yump

Controls:

Z - Select your character
Arrow Keys - Move and Jump

About the game:

A super simple and incomplete platformer game! Currently nothing to do but jump around a single level as three different characters. Really just made this to figure out collisions and basic platformer physics on my own and stopped with that. Probably won't go anywhere else, but if you want, you can download and edit the level in the map.
