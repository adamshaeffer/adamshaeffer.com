Minesweeper 3000

Controls:

Z - Start the game

Expose tile
X - Place flag
Arrow Keys - Move cursor

About the game:

The numbers reveal how many mines are in the eight tiles surrounding that number. Place beacons on all mines, or expose all tiles except the mines to win! Change the number of mines with up/down in the menu.
My version of the classic game: Minesweeper! Styled to look futuristic with fun effects at success and failure! The actual game (excepting visuals and music) only took me two days to build, pretty quick in my opinion! My current high score: 60 mines!
